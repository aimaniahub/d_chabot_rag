## Frequently asked questions

### What services does Darvi Group provide
Premium agricultural solutions for B2B and B2C clients, including:
- Agricultural consultation for small and commercial farms
- IoT-based smart farming solutions
- Sustainable farming practice training and implementation
- Design services and dairy farm management support
- Forestry and high-value plantation guidance including sandalwood

### What agricultural solutions do you offer
Sustainable farming, soil health management, crop optimization, and valuable timber species such as sandalwood. Solutions are designed for quality and practical field use.

### Do you serve commercial farms
Yes. Darvi supports commercial farms and plantations with customized large-scale solutions across Karnataka and neighbouring states.

### How can I register
Use the online form at https://darvigroup.in/form. Provide basic personal and land details and services of interest. Team reviews and contacts the applicant. Current website pricing lists lifetime farmer registration at ₹4,725.

### What documents are needed
Typically Aadhaar, land documents (RTC/survey number), and proof of address. Additional documents may be needed for IoT projects or investment farmland.

### How long does registration processing take
Guidance in knowledge content: usually 2-3 business days for review and verification, then contact for next steps or site visit. Time can vary by location and complexity.

### Do you provide services in my area
Primary operations are in Karnataka, with service to many locations in Karnataka and neighbouring states. Confirm specific locations with the team by phone or contact form.

### What about pricing
- Farmer registration package on website pricing: ₹4,725 lifetime (₹4,500 + GST ₹225)
- Consultation and IoT pricing varies by land size and scope
- Contact team for commercial quotes

### Do you provide after-implementation guidance
Yes. Detailed instructions, follow-up support, training for staff on larger projects, and ongoing consultation options are available.

### What is managed farmland
Agricultural land where professional agronomy teams handle operations from planting to maintenance while investors or owners benefit from asset ownership and structured crop programs. Vikssha Farmland is the managed model promoted in company content.

### Can I visit Vikssha before investing
Knowledge content says site visits can be arranged for serious investors. Contact Darvi Group to schedule.
