## Overview
Darvi Group works on high-value forestry, agroforestry models, and managed farmland investment concepts. A flagship theme in website and knowledge content is Vikssha Farmland: managed farmland designed for long-term value with high-demand plantation crops, professional agronomy management, and investor-friendly amenities.

Website research page: https://darvigroup.in/research
Vikssha / farmland content is also promoted on the main site and SEO content.

## Why managed farmland
Managed farmland means professional teams handle plantation and maintenance operations while the landowner or investor holds the asset. Compared with self-managed normal farmland:
- Crop value focus is higher (premium species)
- Management is professional rather than fully self-dependent
- Income model is structured and more predictable in messaging

## Vikssha Farmland crop portfolio (from knowledge content)
### Indian Sandalwood
- Cultural and commercial value for fragrance and Ayurveda markets
- Lifecycle messaging: roughly 12-15 years maturity pathway in investment content
- Research also covers host plants, growth, size and yield under different soil and climate conditions
- Knowledge content mentions high value heartwood and oil markets

### African Blackwood
- Described as extremely high-value / rare timber with global demand
- Use cases: luxury crafts and instruments
- Knowledge content cites high global price ranges per cubic metre

### Mexican Hass Avocado
- High-yield superfood with export demand
- Yield cycle messaging: initial fruiting around 3-4 years, stronger production later years

Darvi research content highlights sustainable cultivation of premium sandalwood, bamboo as a fast-growing renewable resource, and broader innovative agricultural practices. ## Agroforestry model theme
Company content describes supporting farmers with intercropping systems that generate short-term income while long-gestation trees mature. This is a core story for smallholder-friendly forestry + farming combinations.
