## Agricultural product solutions (knowledge base)
### Genex
- Purpose: enhance soil fertility, improve yield, balance soil pH
- Best for: cereals, vegetables, and general crops
- Typical application guidance in knowledge base: mix about 5 ml per litre water, apply weekly
- Intended results: healthier plants, improved root development, yield support

### Neem
- Purpose: natural pest control for organic-leaning programs
- Best for: vegetables and fruit trees, usable across crops
- Typical application guidance: mix about 3-5 ml per litre, spray bi-weekly
- Focus: reduce harmful insects while remaining safer for beneficial insects when used correctly

### Santica
- Purpose: plant health, productivity, crop resilience
- Best for: fruit trees, flowering plants, high-value crops
- Typical application guidance: mix about 4 ml per litre, apply every 15 days
- Focus: flowering, fruit quality, disease resistance support
