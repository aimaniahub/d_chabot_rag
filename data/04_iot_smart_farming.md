# Darvi Group - IoT and Smart Farming

## Overview
Darvi Group provides IoT-powered agriculture solutions to help farmers monitor land conditions, optimize irrigation and inputs, and make data-driven decisions. Smart farming is positioned as a way to improve yields, reduce waste, and support sustainable operations.

Website: https://darvigroup.in/iot-management
IoT portal referenced in company materials: https://iot.haegl.in

## Key benefits
- Real-time visibility of farm conditions
- Better irrigation and resource efficiency
- Early problem detection for crops and soil
- Remote access from mobile and web
- Data analytics for informed decisions

## Core IoT features
### Real-time monitoring
Track soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and other critical farm metrics through sensor networks.

### Data analytics and smart insights
Use analytics to understand land needs, plan irrigation and fertilization, and improve crop management decisions.

### Sustainable farming
Precision techniques powered by IoT help reduce water waste and improve resource efficiency.

### Remote access
Control and monitor farm systems from anywhere using user-friendly mobile and web applications.

## Solution themes in Darvi knowledge base
### Smart irrigation
Automated watering based on soil moisture and weather context. Aimed at water savings, less manual labour, and optimal plant growth.

### Crop monitoring
Real-time health and issue detection to support targeted interventions and reduce crop loss.

### Weather-related planning
Local monitoring and alerts help plan farm activities and reduce weather-related losses.

### Soil health monitoring
Continuous tracking of moisture and related soil conditions to optimize fertilizer and irrigation use.

## Example impact (from company success content)
A mixed fruit plantation project in Hubli using IoT-based smart farming reduced water consumption by about 30 percent while improving plant health and growth rates through real-time soil moisture guided irrigation.
