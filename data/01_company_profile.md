# Darvi Group - Company Profile

## Introduction
Darvi Group is a B2B and B2C agricultural solutions company. We help farmers and landowners with sustainable farming practices, horticulture and forestry plants, expert consultation, IoT-based smart farming, and managed farmland investments. The company was founded in 2018 and grew from a small agricultural consultancy into a full agricultural solutions provider serving Karnataka and neighbouring states.

## Mission
Revolutionize agricultural practices and forestry management in India. Empower farmers and landowners with modern techniques, sustainable practices, and expert guidance for better yields while protecting natural resources.

## Business summary
Darvi Group provides premium agricultural solutions including sustainable farming practices and valuable timber species such as sandalwood. We serve individual farmers and commercial enterprises. Offerings include agricultural consultation and IoT-based smart farming to optimize crop yields and resource management. Quality standards are maintained so solutions thrive in client environments.
